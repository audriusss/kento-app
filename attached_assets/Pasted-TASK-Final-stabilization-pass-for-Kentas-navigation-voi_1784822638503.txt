STREET RESULT VALIDATION

A Geocoder result is valid only when:

- locality or subAdminArea matches the expected city, when city is known;
- thoroughfare or featureName contains a plausible matching street;
- house number matches through subThoroughfare, featureName, or formatted address;
- latitude and longitude are valid;
- the result is not city-only;
- the result is not a broad administrative area;
- the result is not clearly a city-centre fallback.

Use normalized Lithuanian text comparison:

- lowercase
- trim
- remove punctuation
- tolerate common suffix variants:
  - g.
  - gatvė
  - pr.
  - prospektas
  - al.
  - alėja
  - pl.
  - plentas

Do not require perfect string equality.

Use a simple confidence score instead of one brittle boolean if useful.

Example scoring:

+3 city matches
+3 street strongly matches
+3 house number matches
+1 country matches Lithuania
-5 no street
-5 no number
-5 wrong city

Accept only above a clear threshold.

Keep this logic pure and JVM-testable.

Keep navigation answers deterministic.

Examples:

Distance:

- 1 km:
  "Liko apie vieną kilometrą."
- 2 km:
  "Liko apie du kilometrus."
- 5.6 km:
  "Liko apie penkis su puse kilometro."
- 10 km:
  "Liko apie dešimt kilometrų."

Distance + ETA:
- "Liko apie penkis su puse kilometro. Kelionė truks apie dešimt minučių."

Rules:

- never say "koma";
- never output malformed forms such as "5 koma 6 kilometrai";
- do not use AI chat for route distance or ETA;
- do not add jokes;
- do not add assumptions about dinner, time, mood, traffic, or user intent;
- use one consistent wording path;
- round values sensibly:
  - below 1 km: nearest 50 m
  - 1–20 km: nearest 0.5 km
  - above 20 km: nearest 1 km

Create or reuse pure formatting helpers.
